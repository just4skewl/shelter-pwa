Why This Schedule?
★ Supplies Mon & Wed — Monday resets after the weekend. Wednesday is perfect: supplies arrived Tuesday, count is current for the manager report.
Toilets Mon & Fri — Nicely spaced with Thursday off between them. Tuesday is fully protected for intake.
Mop Wednesday — Already the full-sweep day (supplies + manager report). Floors look fresh for the adoption weekend.
Windows Friday AM — Volunteers do it during 9–11 window. Clean for Saturday's adoption visitors.
🐾 Tuesday, Friday & Saturday protected — Intake day, adoption counselor coverage, busiest adoption day. None carry extra tasks.

9:00–11:00 AM

Delegation Tips
📋 Task card at 9 AM — They only have 2 hours. Hand them a quick card the moment they arrive.
🗺️ Post protocols in each room — Laminated "when a cat leaves" checklist means no interruptions, especially on intake Tuesdays.
⏱️ Stack laundry with cage cleaning — Start a load the moment you arrive. Two loads done before volunteers leave.
🌙 Brief afternoon volunteer at 3:30 immediately — Only 90 minutes. "PM vacuum, poop pickup, trash if full." Done.

Solo Day — Working Smart Without Volunteers

Protect your body, prioritize animal welfare, and let non-critical tasks slide without guilt.

Priority Order
  • 1
    AM medications — before anything else
  • 2
    Spot-clean litter boxes (quick pass)
  • 3
    Start laundry (put a load in, walk away)
  • 4
    Clean counters in highest-traffic rooms only
  • 5
    Vacuum AM — just the priority rooms
  • 6
    Wet food at 12–1 PM
  • 7
    Dishes (quick wash)
  • 8
    PM medications at 3–4 PM
  • 9
    Poop & plate pickup end of day
  • 10
    Set up cart for tomorrow
What Can Slide
✅ PM vacuum — Skip if exhausted. AM round is enough.
✅ Deep litter box cleaning — Spot-clean is fine for one day.
✅ Supply replenishment — Can wait unless something is truly empty.
✅ Mopping / toilets / windows — Never solo-day tasks. Reschedule without guilt.
Ergonomics
🌿 Use your cart to bring supplies to you — not the other way around. Seated break at 11 AM and 3 PM. Alternate standing and kneeling tasks. You can't care for the cats if you wear yourself out.
